reason

Taiwan SAT is the college entrance exam for high school student, but there is no app provide score transform. therefore I made this app for student easily record their score and show those record with nice chart graph.

skill

Use python parser fetching raw data from Taiwan College Entrance Examination Center. Firebase as backend hosting server and auth provider. first time using React-Redux as frontend develop flow.
